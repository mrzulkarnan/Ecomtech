About E-Comm Tech

Bridging the gap between ambition and industry—one student at a time.

Our Story

Founded in 2018 in Lahore, E-Comm Tech started as a small digital-skills boot-camp run out of a 2-room office. Today we train 2,500+ learners every year across English proficiency, IT essentials, e-commerce and freelancing.

Our mission remains simple: make high-impact, career-ready education affordable and accessible to anyone with the drive to learn.

Core Values

Learner First

Curriculum, pacing and support revolve around student success—not the other way around.

Practical Over Theory

Every concept is paired with real-world tasks so you can build a portfolio while you learn.

Continuous Improvement

We update our courses every quarter based on industry feedback and hiring trends.

Affordability

Premium training without premium price-tags—installment plans and scholarships available.
